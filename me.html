Hello!

Thank you for stopping by!

 

I am a Master of Design student at the University of California, Berkeley focusing on design and innovation. I love crafting new forms of human-technology interactions and creating meaningful experiences. I like to call it human-technology interaction and not human-computer interaction because I am interested in exploring interactions beyond screens. I work with digital and physical products, tangible UI experiences and interactive art installations. Before coming to UC Berkeley, I was a Software Engineer at Microsoft India.

While working as a Software Engineer at Microsoft, I had the opportunity to attend numerous tech talks and conferences. After each talk, I would come out feeling drained and defeated. I would find myself thinking "I do not know any of this, I can't do this". But in 2021, I got the opportunity to volunteer and attend Interaction 21. I got to learn about a face of the design would I had never known before. After each talk, I remember thinking - "I do not know any of this, but I want to learn more". I realized that design is where I want to be full-time.

I grew up in a small town in the beautiful state of Kerala, India. I love the rain and green in Kerala. I am an avid traveler, I love traveling mainly because I get to eat different types of food. I am a dessert person to the core. I love painting and my medium is oil. I am still a student, learning new techniques and playing with colors whenever I can. You can find my love for green and rain in most of my paintings as well. The seamless yet subtle interaction between nature and humans is one of the main subjects that I am focusing on now.

Feel free to reach out to me on any of the social media platforms, I am happy to talk about design, art, traveling, food and dessert. If you are in Bay Area, just ping me your favorite dessert place.
