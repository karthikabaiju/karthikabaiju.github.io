Problem Space

Have you ever missed an urgent message from your family while studying in the library?

It's a common problem that many students face. However, checking your phone for messages often leads to getting lost in the endless cycle of social media scrolling, resulting in wasted hours.

Solution

Rumifico and its companion app

Rumifico allows for contextual sharing of moments between parents and college students. Our innovative device, which resembles a pager, allows you to receive messages from the most important people in your life, without getting sucked into the endless cycle of phone use.


Our companion app allows you to configure who can send messages to your Rumifico device and set up quick replies using the three buttons on the device. If you need to send a custom message, you can use the chat function in the companion app to send a message to the other person's Rumifico device. With Rumifico, you can stay connected with your loved ones without getting distracted from your studies.

The Challenge

Identifying the Product Opportunity Gaps and defining the challenge.

We began by exploring many areas for their product, such as memory sharing. We gathered ideas from conferences, research papers, articles, reflective writing, and personal experiences. By using evaluation methods like affinity mapping and weighted matrix, we narrowed down around 50 opportunities to focus on the most promising POG, leading to the current product concept.

Product Requirements: We converted our research findings into aspects our solution must, could, and should have.

The majority of our must-haves fell under emotion and identity. Emotion must-haves centered around the ability to share emotions and feel emotionally at peace, knowing relatives are safe and well. Identity must-haves centered around the idea of 360 contexts to allow users to share when and where events are happening and express that through their own personal lens.

Ideation

We brainstormed 50+ ideas

Each member created approximately 10 concepts using Analogical Reasoning, Forced Analogy, Biomimicry, and other processes from class. This led to a total of 51 ideas.

Narrowing down the ideas

To streamline the ideation process, we used affinity mapping to group similar ideas and identify peripheral concepts that did not align with our POG. This process led to the creation of a "feature graveyard" which listed features unique to eliminated ideas that could be interesting and applicable in the future. Following this, we conducted a dot voting exercise, where each team member was given five votes to assign to the ideas they felt best aligned with our team and personal goals, as well as the POG. After this exercise, we were able to narrow down our list to 14 promising ideas.

VOA & Technology Target

To further refine our selection, we utilized a scoring system based on the number of critical features that each idea fulfilled. After this initial round of evaluation, we plotted the ideas on a target diagram, with the most promising concepts placed at the center to represent their potential for high impact. This target was divided into three levels of digital intensity - fully digital, hybrid, and fully physical. Based on this analysis, we narrowed down our selection to the six most central ideas, which we deemed to have the highest potential impact.

Pugh Chart

A pugh chart was used as a quantitative analysis selection tool with the goal of mitigating bias and ensuring all relevant criteria were considered. All ideas were compared against the baseline design, Interactive Wall Apparatus.

Introducing our final concept

A minimalist device that displays critical messages to loved ones, via visual and tactile alerts. The unique feature of the device is that it can be stuck to any surface like a book, water bottle or key chain. Is is a way to ensure that parents and childeren can stay connected.

How it serves it's purpose?

Functionality

  • Easily communicates availability and unavailability.
  • Messages are completely customizable allowing full self expression.
  • The companion app combined with Rumifico hardware streamlines communication as it allows individuals to filter out all extra notifications and noise a smartphone provides.

USABILITY

  • Nostalgia design allows for individuals who struggle with newer technology to engage seamlessly.
  • Companion app is streamlined to the basic necessities.

VISUAL DESIGN

App Versions
