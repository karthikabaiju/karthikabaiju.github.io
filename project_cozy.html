Personal Project - 2023

Cozy

UC Berkeley

Welcome to Cozy, meditation app and wearable designed to help you find calm in your busy and stressful life. In today's fast-paced world, stress has become a common concern for many individuals, affecting their overall well-being and quality of life. Cozy, along with its wearable keeps track of your stress level and provides a medium to handle the stress. The design of Cozy draws inspiration for the tranquility of rain as it falls on mesh window screen and the vibrations that rain creates.

Role

Concept Generation, Narrative Generation and Visual Design

The Narrative

Building an effective story telling

Designing a story to help users effortlessly understand and connect with what Cozy has to offer. The narrative utilizes calming, poetic and relatable language, fostering a sense of familiarity and ease.

The Design

Wearable

The Cozy wearable, inspired by a mesh window, is designed to be worn on the shoulder. This choice of placement is influenced by the fact that stress often manifests in the shoulders, as the muscles in that area tend to tighten when experiencing stress. By focusing on the shoulders, where tension and discomfort commonly accumulate, the Cozy wearable directly targets the source of stress-related discomfort.

As a stress-monitoring companion, the wearable continuously tracks your heart rate to detect heightened stress levels. When stress is detected, the wearable responds with gentle vibrations, providing a soothing and tactile experience. This subtle vibration acts as a reminder to pause, breathe, and become aware of your stress levels, empowering you to address and alleviate stress in the moment.



The Meditation App

The Cozy app is designed to help you calm down. It provides a visual representation of your stress level on the screen. As your stress level increases, red cracks gradually form on the screen, indicating rising tension. You are prompted to take deep breaths.

As you actively work to calm yourself, the cracks on the screen gradually disappear, revealing a serene rain screen behind them. This transition visually represents the progress you make in reducing stress and finding inner calm.



Moodboards

Stress

Stress is associated with vivid colors like bright red and black, symbolizing intensity and tension. It encompasses a sense of ongoing unease and restlessness.

Calm

Calm is embodied by the deep greens and earthy browns, which symbolize the inherent beauty and tranquility of nature during a gentle rainfall. The moodboard captures the essence of feeling secure and warm indoors, while peacefully observing the rhythmic descent of raindrops through a windowpane.

Wearable

The selection of wearable inspirations was made to capture both the fluidity of rain and the resemblance to mesh windows. These pieces embody the graceful movement and flow evoked by raindrops, while also reflecting the structured elegance found in the design of windows.
